[Sat 6:03pm] Dinner!

[Sat 8:55am] Breakfast!

Good morning, everyone! Hope you have all rested up (or survived the whole night awake) and are ready for another exciting day of hacking! To get you fueled up, breakfast is now ready in the food room (5319), so come over and get a nice early morning energy boost!

[Sat 12:02am] Code in the Dark update

If you're interested in participating in MLH's Code in the Dark competition, it's starting now. Head straight to room 5505.

[Fri 9:59pm] Updates from MLH: Cupcakes and Code in the Dark

Due to a few last-minute logistical changes, MLH will be providing cupcakes at 12 AM on SUNDAY, not 12 AM on Saturday (as in 2 hours from now). In addition, in light of this time change and in hopes of accommodating more people, the Code in the Dark competition will be held at 12 AM on Saturday (so 2 hours from now!) rather than at 3 AM. It will be in room 5505.

[Fri 8:22pm] Need Help? Don't Fret!

If you ever need help with your hacking, whether it's a technical or non-technical issue, don't panic! We have a group of talented and enthusiastic mentors on hand to help you out! Their names and contact info can be found under the Resources section of this page, in the Mentors List. So please do not hesitate to contact an individual mentor via whatever forms of communication they have available, and he/she will be more than happy to assist you! Alternatively, if you are not sure which specific mentor(s) to ask, or if you want just any mentor to be able to investigate your issue and be ready to help you out, you can also fill out the Help Line form, also found under the Resources section of this page. Just indicate your name and hacking location, and type + description of your problem, and any or all of the mentors will be able to see your issue and offer you a hand!

[Fri 7:31pm] Hardware

If you are looking to borrow any piece of hardware, please stop by room 5710. We'll be happy to check out any available hardware to hackers for the entire duration of the hackathon. The full list of hardware that we can lend out to you can be found under the Hardware list in the Resources section of this page. Please remember to bring either a government-issued photo ID or a credit card when you come to the hardware checkout room. Happy hacking!

[Fri 6:17pm] IT'S HAPPENING!!!!!

Finally, the moment you've been waiting for has come, and SteelHacks check-in has kicked off! Come to the 2nd floor of Sennott Square to check in and receive your free t-shirt and some awesome stickers courtesy of Namecheap, Github, and Trello! Once you do, head straight into the auditorium (room 2400) for the opening ceremony, which will start at 7PM. Enjoy some appetizers while you check in and get settled, too :)

[Fri 7:32am] Less than 11 hours left until kickoff!

We are so excited to have you here hacking with us this weekend! Check-in will open at 6 PM in Sennott Square, and the opening ceremony will commence at 7 PM!
