SteelHACKS 2016

FEB 8TH - 10TH, 2016 - PITTSBURGH, PENNSYLVANIA

SteelHACKS is a Hackathon hosted at the University of Pittsburgh for students at colleges & universities across the US who are passionate about getting their hands dirty using technology and design.

Last year was the first incarnation of SteelHACKS. Students from around the Midwest came together to design and build websites and apps.
