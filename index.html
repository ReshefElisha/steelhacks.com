What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is an event in which people, regardless of coding background, come together to make cool stuff. The term "hacking" is used in the traditional sense, which simply means "to create."

Location

SteelHacks will be held at on the University of Pittsburgh campus, in Sennott Square (at 210 South Bouquet Street).

36 Hours of hacking

The hackathon will run for 36 hours from March 27th, 2015 to March 29th, for any university student from the Pittsburgh area.

Registration Deadline

March 20th, 2015, 11:59 PM

Restrictions

Currently, only local university students will be accepted. In future years, we hope that this can change. Additionally, only 150 people total can be registered for this year's SteelHacks.

Team limits

You can have a team of up to 4 people, or not have a team at all. A mixer will be held to pair people that are seeking team mates after the opening ceremony.
